Timeline of Swami Vivekananda
Born Swami Vivekananda born in Culcutta,Bengal India 12 January 1863.
Original name The real name of Swami Vivekananda is Narendranath Dutta.
Family background

  • His mother, Bhuneshwari devi was a devout housewife and father Vishwanath Dutta was an attorney at the Culcutta High Court.
